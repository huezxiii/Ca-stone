Welcome to Clinic X

We offer a wide range of services

Here at Clinic X, we give you quality and affordable dental services. From the simplest up to the most complex dental procedures, we got you covered.

Consultation

During a dental checkup or recall exam, your dentist is trained to look for oral health problems that you cannot always see or feel, problems like deterioration of fillings, signs of gum disease or cancer, and other problems that could affect your general health.

Dental Extraction

Dental extraction, commonly referred to as tooth extraction, is the removal of teeth from the tooth socket. It is the treatment of choice when the tooth is already beyond repair and is infected or when the tooth is extremely mobile due to periodontal disease.

Root Canal Therapy

Root canal treatment is designed to eliminate bacteria from the infected root canal, prevent reinfection of the tooth and save the natural tooth. The infected pulp is removed and the inside of the tooth is cleaned and disinfected, then filled and sealed carefully.

Orthodontist

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that treats and corrects malocclusion. A malocclusion is the misalignment or incorrect relationship of the jaws and teeth. The goal of orthodontic care is to improve a patient's bite. A healthy bite ensures you can eat, chew, and speak properly.

Teeth Restoration

Primary benefit of flouride is that it helps prevent tooth decay. It reverse erosion that has taken place. This treatment can replace the minerals in the enamel that have eroded. These procedures help oral health and function. Common restorations include crowns, bridges and implants

Braces and Dentures

Deciding between getting braces or dentures takes a lot of thought, with the best option for you depending on your dental problem and the severity of it. It is important to discuss any and all possible dental treatments with a specialist to advise you on the best course of action to take.
